State Solar Subsidies in India

Explore state-wise rooftop solar incentives, eligibility, and application guidance. Some states provide additional benefits beyond central government programs, while others primarily follow national schemes.

What are State Solar Subsidies?

In addition to central government programs, some Indian states may offer their own rooftop solar incentives or support mechanisms. The availability and details vary by state and can change over time.

Before planning a rooftop solar installation, check the latest information from your State Nodal Agency or local electricity distribution company (DISCOM).

Popular States

🌾 West Bengal

West Bengal primarily follows the Central Government rooftop solar subsidy program. Consumers should also check with WBSEDCL or their local DISCOM for the latest updates and eligibility.

☀ Gujarat

Gujarat has been one of the leading states in rooftop solar adoption. Residents should verify the latest subsidy and net-metering rules through the state authorities.

⚡ Maharashtra

Consumers can benefit from central rooftop solar schemes, subject to the latest regulations and eligibility notified by the implementing agencies.

🏜 Rajasthan

Rajasthan promotes solar energy because of its high solar potential. Check the State Nodal Agency for current rooftop solar policies and incentives.

🌞 Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu supports renewable energy development. Rooftop solar consumers should verify the latest subsidy and net-metering provisions before applying.

🏡 Karnataka

Karnataka has a strong renewable energy sector. Availability of rooftop solar benefits depends on current government and DISCOM guidelines.

Important Information

Solar subsidy availability differs from state to state. While some states may introduce additional incentives, others mainly implement the central government rooftop solar program.

Before purchasing a solar system, always verify the latest subsidy amount, eligibility, approved vendors, and application process through your State Nodal Agency or electricity distribution company.

General Eligibility

  • Residential consumers with a valid electricity connection.
  • Ownership or authorized use of the installation property.
  • Installation through an approved vendor, where required.
  • Compliance with applicable state and central guidelines.
  • Completion of all required documentation.

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card or other accepted identity proof.
  • Latest electricity bill.
  • Address proof.
  • Property ownership proof (if applicable).
  • Recent passport-size photograph.
  • Bank account details for subsidy disbursement.
  • Mobile number and email address.

How to Apply

  1. Check whether your state offers any additional rooftop solar benefits.
  2. Verify your eligibility under the applicable state and central government schemes.
  3. Select an approved solar installation vendor, if required.
  4. Submit your application through the designated government portal or State Nodal Agency.
  5. Upload all required documents.
  6. Wait for document verification and approval.
  7. Install the solar system after receiving the necessary approval.
  8. Complete inspection and commissioning as per the applicable guidelines.
  9. If eligible, the subsidy will be processed according to the prevailing government procedures.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do all states provide additional solar subsidies?

No. Some states provide additional incentives, while others primarily follow the Central Government rooftop solar subsidy program.

Can subsidy rules change?

Yes. Subsidy amounts, eligibility criteria, approved vendors, and application procedures may change over time.

Where can I verify the latest information?

You should check with your State Nodal Agency, local DISCOM, or the official government rooftop solar portal for the latest updates.

Can I receive both state and central benefits?

This depends on the current policies applicable in your state. Always review the latest official guidelines before applying.

Do I need an approved vendor?

Many subsidy programs require installation through approved vendors. Verify the latest requirements before proceeding.
