About SolarPedia

Empowering people with reliable solar knowledge, practical guides, and the latest renewable energy updates.

Who We Are

SolarPedia is an independent educational platform dedicated to helping homeowners, students, businesses, and anyone interested in solar energy. Our goal is to make solar information easy to understand through practical guides, news, and educational articles.

Our Mission

Our mission is to increase awareness of renewable energy by providing accurate, easy-to-read, and regularly updated information about rooftop solar systems, government schemes, solar technology, and energy-saving solutions.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every household has access to clear, trustworthy information that helps them make informed decisions about clean energy.
