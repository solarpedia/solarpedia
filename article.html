PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana – Complete Guide

✍️ SolarPedia Team 📅 18 July 2026 ⏱️ 8 min read

Table of Contents

  • Introduction
  • Eligibility
  • Documents
  • Application Process
  • FAQ

Introduction

PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana is a Government of India initiative to encourage residential rooftop solar adoption by providing eligible households with financial assistance toward installation costs. Always refer to the official scheme portal for the latest eligibility criteria and subsidy details.

Who Can Apply?

To apply for the PM Surya Ghar rooftop solar scheme, the applicant should generally:

  • Be an Indian residential electricity consumer.
  • Have a valid electricity connection.
  • Own the rooftop or have permission to install the system.
  • Meet the latest scheme requirements published by the government.
Tip: Always verify the latest eligibility rules on the official PM Surya Ghar portal before applying.

Required Documents

You may be asked to provide documents such as:

Document Purpose
Aadhaar Card Identity verification
Electricity Bill Consumer details
Bank Passbook Subsidy transfer
Passport Photo Application record

Application Process

  1. Register on the official portal.
  2. Submit consumer details.
  3. Select a registered solar vendor.
  4. Install the rooftop solar system.
  5. Complete inspection and net-metering (where applicable).
  6. Receive the subsidy after approval, according to the current government process.
Important: Do not pay money to unofficial agents promising guaranteed approval. Always use the official government portal and registered vendors.

Benefits of Rooftop Solar

  • Lower electricity bills.
  • Clean renewable energy.
  • Reduced carbon emissions.
  • Long operational life with proper maintenance.
  • Can increase property value.

Conclusion

Rooftop solar can be a worthwhile investment for many households. Before making a decision, compare system sizes, understand the subsidy process, and consult an authorized installer. Always rely on official government information for the latest scheme rules and subsidy amounts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for the PM Surya Ghar Scheme?

Residential electricity consumers who meet the latest government eligibility criteria can apply.

Is the subsidy available across India?

The scheme is intended for eligible households across India, subject to the current government guidelines.

How long does rooftop solar last?

Most quality solar panels have a lifespan of 25 years or more with proper maintenance.
