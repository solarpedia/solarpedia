Introduction

PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana is a Government of India initiative to encourage residential rooftop solar adoption by providing eligible households with financial assistance toward installation costs. Always refer to the official scheme portal for the latest eligibility criteria and subsidy details.
