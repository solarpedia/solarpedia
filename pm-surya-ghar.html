PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana

A flagship rooftop solar scheme by the Government of India that helps households install solar panels with financial assistance to reduce electricity bills and promote clean energy.

What is PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana?

PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana is a Government of India initiative launched to encourage residential rooftop solar adoption. The scheme provides financial assistance for eligible households installing grid-connected rooftop solar systems.

The objective is to help families reduce electricity expenses, promote renewable energy, and increase the use of clean power across the country.

By generating electricity from sunlight, homeowners can lower their dependence on conventional electricity and contribute to a greener future.

Key Benefits

💰 Financial Assistance

Eligible residential consumers can receive financial assistance from the Government of India for installing grid-connected rooftop solar systems, subject to the latest scheme guidelines.

⚡ Lower Electricity Bills

Generate your own electricity during the day to reduce dependence on the grid and lower monthly electricity bills.

🌱 Clean Energy

Solar energy is renewable and environmentally friendly, helping reduce carbon emissions and reliance on fossil fuels.

🏠 Increase Property Value

Homes with rooftop solar systems may benefit from reduced operating costs and can be more attractive to some buyers.

🔋 Long-Term Savings

Although there is an initial installation cost, rooftop solar systems can provide savings over many years through reduced electricity expenses.

📈 Support Renewable Energy

Installing rooftop solar contributes to India's renewable energy goals and encourages sustainable electricity generation.

Subsidy Information

The subsidy amount depends on the approved scheme guidelines, your installed rooftop solar capacity, and the applicable government rules at the time of application.

Because subsidy rates can change, always verify the latest details on the official government portal before making a purchase decision.

Solar System Size Subsidy Status
Up to 2 kW As per current Government guidelines
Above 2 kW As per applicable scheme rules
Maximum Eligible Capacity Subject to the latest official scheme provisions

Eligibility

  • Applicant should be a residential electricity consumer.
  • The property should have adequate rooftop space.
  • The rooftop solar system should comply with applicable standards.
  • Installation should follow the requirements of the local electricity distribution company (DISCOM).
  • Applicants must satisfy the eligibility conditions under the latest government guidelines.

Documents Required

  • Identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, or other accepted ID)
  • Address proof
  • Latest electricity bill
  • Property ownership or authorization documents (if applicable)
  • Passport-size photograph (if required)
  • Bank account details for subsidy processing (as applicable)

Application Process

  1. Check your eligibility under the current scheme guidelines.
  2. Register through the official government portal.
  3. Select an approved vendor.
  4. Install the rooftop solar system.
  5. Complete inspection and net metering (where applicable).
  6. Receive subsidy after successful verification, if eligible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana?

Residential electricity consumers who meet the latest eligibility requirements of the scheme and their local electricity distribution company (DISCOM) can apply.

Can I install the solar system myself?

The installation process should follow the applicable scheme guidelines and technical requirements. Using an approved vendor may be necessary depending on the current program rules.

How long does the application process take?

The timeline varies depending on document verification, installation, inspection, and approval by the concerned authorities.

Will I still receive an electricity bill?

Yes. Your electricity bill depends on your energy consumption, solar generation, and the applicable billing and net metering rules.

Where can I check the latest subsidy rules?

Always refer to the official PM Surya Ghar portal and your local DISCOM for the latest subsidy amounts, eligibility criteria, and application procedures.
